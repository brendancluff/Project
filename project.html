About the Academy

Skyborn Academy has trained the realm’s most skilled dragon riders for centuries. Nestled atop the ancient cliffs of Aeryndor, the academy stands as both a fortress and a sanctuary for those chosen to bond with the mighty winged guardians of the skies. Founded during the Age of Embers, when dragons first allied with humankind, Skyborn quickly rose to prominence as the most respected institution of aerial mastery. Over the ages, its halls have echoed with the footsteps of kings, war heroes, and legendary riders whose names are etched in the annals of history. The academy’s rigorous training blends ancient traditions with evolving techniques, ensuring that each graduate is not only a skilled rider, but a leader of honor and courage. To earn the title of Skyborn Rider is to hold a place among the elite — a symbol of prestige recognized across all the realms.

What Awaits You

  • Training Grounds: Hone your skills in aerial maneuvers and dragon handling.
  • Dragon Stables: Bond with dragons from hatchling to full-grown companion.
  • Graduation Ceremony: Earn your rider’s crest in a time-honored ritual.
