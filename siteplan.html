Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in The Skyborn Academy?

You can enroll by visiting our Contact section and reaching out to our admissions team. We’ll guide you through the application and bonding evaluation process.

Do I need to have my own dragon to apply?

No, dragons will be assigned to students based on compatibility assessments during the first month of training.

Is housing provided for students?

Yes, students are provided with on-campus lodging, including dragon-friendly shelters.

How long is the training program?

Our standard training program spans two years, with options for extended advanced studies and specializations.
